Guess the SAYDLE in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Click the Record button to record audio and say the word out loud.

In case of homophones, try saying a sentence with target word being the first word.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
